Privacy Policy - AstroWear

Last updated: August 2025

Overview

AstroWear is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our Wear OS game handles your information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

AstroWear is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics services that track user behavior
  • No Advertising: Our game contains no advertisements and no ad tracking
  • No Network Access: The game works completely offline and does not access the internet
  • No Location Data: We do not access or use your location information
  • No Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers or hardware information

Local Data Storage

AstroWear only stores data locally on your device:

  • Game Settings: Your preferences (sound, haptic feedback, etc.) are stored locally
  • High Scores: Your best scores are saved locally on your device only
  • Game Progress: Current game state is temporarily stored during play

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Permissions

AstroWear requires minimal permissions:

  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback during gameplay (optional, can be disabled)
  • Wake Lock: Prevents screen from turning off during active gameplay

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there are no data security concerns. All game data remains securely on your device under Android's standard app sandboxing.

Children's Privacy

AstroWear is safe for children of all ages. We do not collect any information from children or adults. The game operates entirely offline without any data collection.

Third-Party Services

AstroWear does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Data Deletion

To delete all game data:

  1. Uninstall the AstroWear app from your Wear OS device
  2. All local game data (settings, high scores) will be automatically removed

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Since the app operates offline, we recommend checking this page periodically for updates.

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Google Play Store requirements
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
