Privacy Policy - Takohi Apps

Developer: Takohi (KonsomeJona)
Contact: konsomejona@gmail.com
Website: konsomejona.github.io

Your Privacy is Protected

Takohi apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Our games and applications run locally on your device.

Scope of This Policy

This privacy policy applies to all applications published by Takohi (KonsomeJona) on Google Play Store and other platforms, including but not limited to games for Wear OS, Android phones, and tablets.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Takohi apps are designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or tracking data
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Game scores, achievements, or progress (except locally on your device)
  • Any form of user data transmitted to external servers

How Our Apps Work

Takohi applications:

  • Run entirely on your device
  • Do not require internet connectivity for core functionality
  • Store preferences and game data locally on your device only
  • Do not sync data between devices or to cloud services
  • Do not have user accounts or profiles

Local Data Storage

Our apps may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Game preferences and settings
  • High scores and statistics
  • Progress and achievements

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be accessed by us or any third party

In-App Purchases

Some Takohi apps may offer optional in-app purchases through the Google Play Store. When you make a purchase:

  • The transaction is processed entirely by Google Play
  • We do not receive or store your payment information
  • Google Play handles all billing securely
  • Your purchase status is stored locally on your device

For information about how Google handles payment data, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Takohi apps may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing optional in-app purchases (handled entirely by Google)

Our apps do NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud services for data storage
  • Crash reporting services that transmit data

Permissions

Our apps only request the minimal permissions necessary to function. Common permissions include:

  • Vibration: For haptic feedback during gameplay
  • Wake Lock: To keep the screen on during active use
  • Billing: For processing optional in-app purchases

We never request permissions for accessing contacts, camera, microphone, location, or other sensitive data unless absolutely required for the app's core functionality.

Children's Privacy

Takohi apps are designed to be safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, our apps are safe for children to use without parental consent concerns related to data collection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
