Privacy Policy

Farkle - Dice Game for Wear OS
Last updated: March 15, 2026
Farkle is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information Collection

Farkle does not collect any personal information. The app:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or any identifying information
  • Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services
  • Does not access your contacts, camera, location, or other sensitive device features
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers

Microphone Permission

The app may request the RECORD_AUDIO permission to enable optional voice input for player names or in-game actions. Voice data is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

Data Storage

Farkle stores the following data locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Game progress and scores
  • User preferences and settings
  • In-app purchase status

This data remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted externally. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Play Billing to process in-app purchases. When you make a purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information. Google's handling of your data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools are integrated into the app.

Children's Privacy

Farkle does not collect personal information from any user, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
