Permissions Used

WearGB requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Audio Output: To play Game Boy audio through your watch speaker
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback when pressing game controls
  • Network (Optional): Only for ROM downloading feature - can be disabled

Legal ROM Requirements

WearGB does not include any copyrighted game files. Users must:

  • Provide their own legally obtained ROM files
  • Ensure they own the physical cartridges for any ROM files used
  • Comply with local copyright laws regarding game emulation
  • Understand that downloading copyrighted ROMs without ownership may be illegal
