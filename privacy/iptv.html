Privacy Policy for IPTV WearOS App

Last Updated: September 3, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how IPTV WearOS App ("we", "our", or "the app") developed by KonsomeJona handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • IPTV Playlist URLs: URLs you enter to access IPTV content
  • Account Names: Custom names you assign to your IPTV accounts
  • Favorite Channels: Channels you mark as favorites within the app

Automatically Collected Information

  • App Usage Data: Basic analytics about app performance and crashes (via Firebase)
  • Device Information: WearOS version, device model for compatibility purposes

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Enable IPTV streaming functionality
  • Save your playlists and favorites locally on your device
  • Sync data between your paired devices (WearOS, Mobile, TV)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Ensure compatibility with your device

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All IPTV URLs, account data, and favorites are stored locally on your device
  • Data is encrypted using Android's built-in security features
  • No IPTV credentials or streaming data is sent to our servers

Firebase Services

We use Firebase for:

  • QR code functionality (temporary session data, deleted after use)
  • Anonymous crash reporting and performance monitoring

Firebase data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • Store your IPTV credentials on our servers
  • Access or view your IPTV content or playlists
  • Track your viewing habits or channel preferences

We only share data when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect rights, property, or safety
  • You explicitly consent to sharing

Cross-Device Synchronization

  • Sync occurs only between your own paired devices
  • Data transfer happens through encrypted channels
  • You can disable sync at any time in settings
  • Synced data remains under your control

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Delete Data: Remove all accounts and data from the app at any time
  • Disable Sync: Turn off cross-device synchronization
  • Clear Favorites: Remove all favorite channels
  • Uninstall: Complete data removal by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

The app may contain links to IPTV services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Google Play Store policies
  • WearOS platform requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable

Consent
