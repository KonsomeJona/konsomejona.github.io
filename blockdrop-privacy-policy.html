BlockDrop Privacy Policy

App Name: BlockDrop
Package ID: me.takohi.blockdrop
Developer: Takohi (KonsomeJona)
Platform: Wear OS
Contact: konsomejona@gmail.com

Your Privacy is Protected

BlockDrop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

This is a completely offline game that runs locally on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

BlockDrop is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or tracking data
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Game scores or achievements
  • Any form of user data

How BlockDrop Works

BlockDrop is a classic block-stacking puzzle game that:

  • Runs entirely on your Wear OS device
  • Does not require internet connectivity for gameplay
  • Stores game preferences and high scores locally on your device only
  • Does not sync data between devices
  • Does not have user accounts or profiles

Data Storage

The only data stored by BlockDrop are your game preferences, high scores, and statistics, which are saved locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be accessed by us or any third party

In-App Purchases

BlockDrop offers an optional in-app purchase ("Supporter" upgrade) through the Google Play Store. When you make a purchase:

  • The transaction is processed entirely by Google Play
  • We do not receive or store your payment information
  • Google Play handles all billing securely
  • Your purchase status is stored locally on your device

For information about how Google handles payment data, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

BlockDrop uses only the following third-party service:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing optional in-app purchases (handled entirely by Google)

BlockDrop does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud services for data storage

Permissions

BlockDrop only requests the minimal permissions necessary to function:

  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback during gameplay (optional, can be disabled in settings)
  • Wake Lock: Keeps the screen on during active gameplay
  • Billing: Required for processing optional in-app purchases through Google Play

Children's Privacy

BlockDrop is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the game is safe for children to play without parental consent concerns related to data collection.
