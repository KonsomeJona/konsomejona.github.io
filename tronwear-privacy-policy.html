TronWear does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

This is a completely offline game that runs locally on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

TronWear is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or tracking data
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Game scores or achievements
  • Any form of user data

How TronWear Works

TronWear is a classic light cycle game that:

  • Runs entirely on your Wear OS device
  • Does not require internet connectivity
  • Stores game preferences locally on your device only
  • Does not sync data between devices
  • Does not have user accounts or profiles

Data Storage

The only data stored by TronWear are your game preferences (such as difficulty level and sound settings), which are saved locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be accessed by us or any third party

Third-Party Services

TronWear does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud services

Permissions

TronWear only requests the minimal permissions necessary to function:

  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback during gameplay
  • Wake Lock: Keeps the screen on during active gameplay

Children's Privacy

TronWear is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the game is safe for children to play without parental consent concerns related to data collection.
