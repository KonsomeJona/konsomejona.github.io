Privacy Policy - Time Speak (Hourly Chime)

Last updated: January 31, 2025

App Package: me.takohi.hourlychime | Developer: Takohi

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Time Speak (Hourly Chime) app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices and wearables that was created by Takohi (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free app. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

This privacy policy is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

2. Information Collection and Use

Time Speak does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data. The Application operates entirely offline on your device without any internet connectivity, servers, or cloud services.

2.1 No Data Collection

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data (GPS, network-based location)
  • Contact information or address book data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, advertising ID)
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Crash reports or performance data
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Calendar, SMS, or call log data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Installed apps information

2.2 Local Data Storage Only

The Application stores minimal configuration data locally using Android SharedPreferences:

  • Hourly chime enabled/disabled state
  • Quiet hours settings (start and end time)
  • Voice customization preferences (pitch, speed)
  • Vibration settings
  • Language preference for announcements
  • Respect ringer mode preference

This data is stored securely on your device, is never transmitted anywhere, and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Required Permissions Explained

The Application requires certain permissions to function properly. Here's exactly why each permission is needed:

Permission Purpose Data Access
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Display time announcement notifications No data collected
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM Schedule precise hourly chimes No data collected
USE_EXACT_ALARM Ensure timely announcements No data collected
VIBRATE Provide haptic feedback No data collected
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Restart service after device reboot No data collected
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Keep service running reliably No data collected
WAKE_LOCK Announce time when screen is off No data collected
MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS Adjust volume for announcements No data collected

4. Third Party Services

The Application does not use any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks (AdMob, Facebook Ads, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools (Crashlytics, Bugsnag, etc.)
  • Social media SDKs
  • Payment processors
  • Cloud storage services
  • Push notification services

5. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, since the Application does not collect or transmit any data, there are no security risks related to data breaches, unauthorized access, or data transmission.

The minimal preferences stored locally are protected by Android's built-in security features and app sandboxing.

6. Children's Privacy

These Services are suitable for all ages. The Application does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app does not require age verification as no data is collected.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware of any data collection occurring, please contact us immediately as this would be contrary to our stated practices.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to retain or delete on our servers. Local preferences stored on your device are:

  • Retained only while the app is installed
  • Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Can be cleared through Android's app settings (Clear Data)
  • Not backed up to Google Drive or any cloud service

8. User Rights

Under various privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), users have certain rights regarding their data. Since we don't collect any data:

  • Right to Access: There is no data to access
  • Right to Rectification: There is no data to correct
  • Right to Erasure: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Right to Data Portability: Not applicable as no data is collected
  • Right to Object: Not applicable as no processing occurs

9. International Users

The Application is available worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international data transfer concerns. The app operates entirely locally on your device regardless of your location.
