ZenBreath stores all data locally on your device only.

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal information or meditation data.

Information Collection and Use

ZenBreath is designed to respect your privacy. All data remains on your Wear OS device:

  • Breathing Sessions: Start time, duration, cycles completed, and ratings
  • Settings: Your preferences for duration, vibration, and sound
  • Statistics: Daily session count and total breathing time

Important: This data is stored locally using Android's Room database and SharedPreferences. It never leaves your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

ZenBreath does not collect or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or unique IDs
  • Location data
  • Health sensor data (heart rate, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data to external servers

How ZenBreath Works

ZenBreath is a mindful breathing companion that:

  • Runs entirely on your Wear OS device
  • Works completely offline (no internet required for core features)
  • Stores your meditation history locally only
  • Does not sync data between devices
  • Does not require user accounts or sign-in

Permissions Used

ZenBreath requests only the permissions necessary for its features:

VIBRATE: Provides haptic feedback during breathing exercises to guide your breath
WAKE_LOCK: Keeps the screen on during active breathing sessions
INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Only used when you tap the support button to open the donation page on your connected phone
POST_NOTIFICATIONS: For optional session reminders (feature in development)

Third-Party Services

ZenBreath does not integrate with any third-party services for data collection:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud backup services
  • No crash reporting services

Data Security

Your meditation data is protected by:

  • Android's built-in app sandboxing
  • Local storage that requires physical device access
  • No network transmission of personal data
  • Automatic data deletion when app is uninstalled

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data in the app's History screen
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
  • Portability: Data remains on your device only
  • No Sharing: Your data is never shared with anyone

Children's Privacy

ZenBreath is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information online, the app is safe for children under 13 to use without parental consent concerns related to data collection.

International Users

ZenBreath stores all data locally on your device, regardless of your location. No data is transmitted internationally.
