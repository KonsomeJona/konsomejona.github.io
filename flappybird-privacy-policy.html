FlappyBird does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

This is a completely offline game that runs locally on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

FlappyBird is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or tracking data
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Game scores or achievements
  • Any form of user data

How FlappyBird Works

FlappyBird is a classic endless runner game that:

  • Runs entirely on your Wear OS device
  • Does not require internet connectivity
  • Stores game preferences and high scores locally on your device only
  • Does not sync data between devices
  • Does not have user accounts or profiles

Data Storage

The only data stored by FlappyBird are your game preferences and high scores, which are saved locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Cannot be accessed by us or any third party

Third-Party Services

FlappyBird does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud services

Permissions

FlappyBird only requests the minimal permissions necessary to function:

  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback during gameplay (optional, can be disabled)
  • Wake Lock: Keeps the screen on during active gameplay

Children's Privacy

FlappyBird is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the game is safe for children to play without parental consent concerns related to data collection.
